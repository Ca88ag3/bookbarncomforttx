Comfort TX Book Barn

About Us

Not Barnes and Noble, just a noble barn. With an incredible selection of used books across a wide variety of genres, you're bound to find something you'll enjoy. Run by volunteers and friends of Comfort Public Library. All sales are by donation and 100% of proceeds go directly to the library! We're open 10am-2pm every Saturday (later if busy or during community events). Check it out, tell your friends, and come find some great books at The Book Barn in Comfort TX.
